About Us

We're students of the "Multimedia and New Services" subject, and our aim was to make a dynamic website filled with multimedia content. For this purpose, we chose Guild Wars 2 as our theme because of its interesting visuals and engaging gameplay mechanics.
What follows is our own version of what a multimedia-filled fansite would look like.

Races

The world of Guild Wars 2 is filled with exotic races with a deep lore and story. However, the playable races are restricted to five:
the snarky asura, the curious sylvari, the ferocious charr, the boasting norn and the resilient humans.
